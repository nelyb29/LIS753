Buy your tickets now!

Customers will be originally admitted in the order of their numbered tickets that are given out. Due to our severe space limitations and compelling issues of health and safety, we will only allow 160 customers into the Main Room at a time.

You can purchase gift certificates at the sale in $5 denominations.

Tickets are important

Some customers come very early on Saturday to ensure an early place in line. We hand out a maximum of two numbered tickets per person at the Main Room, beginning at 8 am on the Saturday of the sale. If you receive a ticket, the number on it reserves your corresponding place in the line, allowing you to go off and get coffee or to visit the Children's and Bargain Book rooms. Please be sure to return to the Main Room a few minutes before it opens to reclaim your spot in the line: if you miss the time when your number is allowed to enter the Main Room you will forfeit your place in line.
